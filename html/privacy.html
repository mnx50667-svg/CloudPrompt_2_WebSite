Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

CloudPrompt is committed to protecting your privacy. Your prompts are stored in YOUR Google Drive. We never see or access your data.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your prompts or saved content
  • Your Google Drive files
  • Your browsing history
  • Personal information beyond authentication

How Your Data Is Stored

All prompts are stored in your personal Google Drive in a folder called "CloudPrompt". You have complete control over your data and can access, modify, or delete it directly.

Permissions

We request only minimum permissions: local storage for caching and limited Google Drive access for files we create. We cannot access your other Drive files.
